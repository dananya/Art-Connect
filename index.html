Artconnect aims at bridging the gap between the artisans and the corporate world and looks forward to a holistic development. The artisans post their queries, about their difficulties and if they need any assistance with anything. The organisations/companies can contact the artisans through the website and provide them with the required help. The companies/organisations also reach out to the artisans through the website, for the latter's help in promoting their campaign, helping with the packaging and also spreading a word, if need be. This website provides a source of relief for the artisans.
